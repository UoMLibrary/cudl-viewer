Launching the Sanskrit and Spanish Chapbook Collections

We have added more items to the Islamic Manuscripts and Newton collections - and have initiated two new collections: the Sanskrit Manuscripts and Spanish Chapbooks. Both these collections will grow over the next two years.

We are also continuing to improve functionality, allowing you to search within transcribed manuscripts, displaying more information about each document and making bookmarking an individual page easier.
